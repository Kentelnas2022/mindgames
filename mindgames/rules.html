Welcome to the Memory Game! Your objective is to match all the pairs of emojis on the board.

How to Play?

Click on any face-down card to reveal the emoji symbol. Click on another face-down card to reveal its symbol. If the symbols on the two cards match, they remain face-up. If the symbols do not match, both cards are turned face-down again after a brief moment. Continue flipping pairs of cards until all pairs are matched. The game ends when all pairs are successfully matched, or the time limit is reached. If all pairs are matched within the time limit, you win the game. If you run out of time before all pairs are matched, the game ends. Click the "Try Again" button to restart the game and try to beat your previous score.

Tips

Pay attention to the location of each emoji as you flip cards to improve your memory. Try to complete the game with the fewest moves and shortest time to achieve a high score. Practice regularly to improve your memory and speed. Enjoy the challenge of matching emojis in this memory game!
